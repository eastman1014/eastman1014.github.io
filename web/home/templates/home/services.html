Deluxe Exterior Service Procedure

Step 1 Pre-Wash

In the Pre-wash stage, we thoroughly rinse all dirt and grime that sticks to tires, rims, trim, bumpers, and windows. We focus on these areas with our pressure washer to minimize dirt, stains, sand, insects, etc.

Step 2 Foam Wash

In this step we equip our foam/soap cannon attachment to a pressure washer, and this covers the vehicle in a super cleaning formula that removes all stains or dirt. We then hand wash the exterior of the car to ensure a guaranteed shine. After we thoroughly sprayed and hand-washed the vehicle, we then immediately rinse and dry every panel of the car to prevent water streaks.

Step 3 Tire and Rim Treatment

At this time, we spray the tires and rims with a special cleansing shampoo. The special cleansing shampoo allows us to deeply scrub the tire and rims to remove all further stains. This fully restores the color of your tires and the shine your rims deserve!

Step 4 Trim Restoration

In this Florida heat, your vehicle's trim is becoming more discolored due to an excess of sunlight exposure.We add a protectant layer of a thin plastic coating that we spray on each exterior trim panel that restores color.

Step 5 Headlight Restoration

Over time your headlights will sustain fog and yellow tinting which is primarily because of the sun, rain, pollen, etc. We have a full headlight restoration kit that clears and prevents any yellow tinting on the headlight surface.

Step 6 Polish and Wax

And finally, we have polish buffing and wax buffing by hand to ensure high-quality definition and shine on your vehicle. One of the best aspects about our polish and wax buffing service is that it reduces any scratches or scruffs and provides a protectant layer to prevent any paint damage or deterioration due to sun, rain, insects, etc. (it will not protect your vehicle from rocks or other vehicles).
